## The Story

Scott is building two things at the same time, and they are not separate projects — they are the same project at different scales.

AESOP AI Academy exists because AI is already reshaping how people work, learn, and relate to each other, and most people don't see it coming. Not because they aren't intelligent, but because nobody has taken the time to explain it to them honestly — without hype, without condescension, without an agenda to sell them something. The people who understand AI are mostly the people who built it. Everyone else is navigating it blind.

Scott's bet is that this gap has consequences. Not abstract ones — real ones. Psychological ones. Cultural ones. Governance ones. Youth entering the workforce now will inherit an AI-saturated world that their education never prepared them for. That's not a technology problem. It's a literacy problem. And literacy problems have always been solved the same way: someone decides to teach.

That's AESOP. Free. Rigorous. Built for the people who weren't handed the keys.

## Why Polaris

You can't build a large-scale education platform alone, working in a browser tab, one AI session at a time. The ambition outpaces the tooling.

Polaris is how Scott gets more done. Multiple agents running simultaneously. Real API control. A clean window instead of a cluttered browser. A coding environment that is flexible, efficient, and built around the way he actually works — not the way a product team decided he should.

It is also something he intends to give away. Board members who are technically inclined. Coders he meets who are looking for a better environment. A packaged executable they can install and run. The same instinct that built AESOP — if it helps me, it can help others, so let's make it shareable.

## Why Scott

He has the time and he has the passion. That is the whole answer, and it is enough. Most things that matter get built by people who show up consistently without being paid to. Scott is that person for this work.
