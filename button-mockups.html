STYLE:
1. Glassmorphic
Frosted glass with backdrop blur, subtle inner glow on top edge, light tint per category. Modern, calm, expensive-feeling. Hover lifts the button slightly. Best in dark themes; the blur effect needs something behind it to look good.
2. Neumorphic (Soft 3D Pillows)
Soft pillowy buttons with two-tone shadows (dark below-right, light above-left). Color expressed through glowing text rather than fill. Tactile, calm, almost dreamy. Pressed state inverts to look depressed. Trendy 2020-style.
3. Glossy Aqua (macOS-style)
Pill-shaped, vertical gradient fill, top-half overlay creates the "wet" glass highlight. Throwback to early-2000s macOS. Highly polished and recognizable. Risks looking dated.
4. Material Elevation
Flat fills with stacked drop shadows that lift on hover. Uppercase text with letter-spacing. Confident, conventional, very Google Material. Easy to read, predictable, slightly corporate.
5. Gradient Pill
Pill-shaped buttons with diagonal gradient fills. Bright, energetic, scales up on hover. Reads as friendly and modern. Great for marketing pages, may feel a bit playful for a developer tool.
6. Animated Border Glow
Dark interior with an animated rainbow gradient border that slowly rotates around the perimeter. Eye-catching but not hyperactive. Subtle by default, mesmerizing on careful inspection.
7. Cyberpunk Neon
Angular slashed corners (clip-path), monospace uppercase text, neon glow, dark interior. Maximalist hacker aesthetic. Strong opinion — you either love this or hate it.
8. Brushed Steel
Repeating vertical lines simulating a brushed metal surface, with a top-to-bottom darkening gradient. Industrial, professional, slightly retro. Color expressed through text only.
9. 3D Extruded
Solid color top, dark same-hue side face, ground shadow. Looks like the button stands up off the page. Press it and it sinks. Tactile, fun, very gameified — could make Polaris feel less serious.
10. Holographic / Liquid
Multi-color gradients that slowly shift around the button. Premium, futuristic, slightly aurora-like. Each tint family blends into adjacent hues so it feels alive without distracting.
11. Neumorphic + Holographic Text
Soft 3D pillow body from style #2, but the text itself is the animated multi-color gradient from style #10 — calm structure with subtly alive labels. The button itself sits still; the colors flow inside the words. Best in dark themes.
